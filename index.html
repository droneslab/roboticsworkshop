SUNY Robotics & Autonomy Workshop

Developing a roadmap for robotics education and research across New York State

August 7-8, 2025

Workshop Purpose

This workshop brings together stakeholders in robotics, autonomy, and embodied intelligence across New York State, including:

  • Researchers and educators from SUNY campuses
  • Industry leaders from NY and beyond
  • State and federal representatives
  • K-12 STEM educators

We aim to develop a holistic roadmap to address the upcoming wave of robotics and autonomy that will transform sectors including manufacturing, defense/aerospace, agriculture, energy, and healthcare.

Roadmap Objectives

Education & Research

Inform SUNY and NYS about future programs to address rapid growth in robotics and embodied intelligence.

Student & Industry Needs

Guide SUNY campuses on meeting upcoming needs for students and industry personnel (reskilling/upskilling).

Collaboration Platform

Develop a shared platform across campuses for continued interaction and resource development.

We seek your participation to:

  • Inform us of your needs
  • Provide feedback on our vision
  • Identify gaps in current research/education/partnerships
